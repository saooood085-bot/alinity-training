🎬 Abbott Official Introduction
About This Course

Why Train on the Alinity ci Platform?

The Alinity c and i are Abbott's flagship analyzers for clinical chemistry and immunoassay. This course equips you with everything needed for confident, safe, and efficient operation.

🔬

Clinical Chemistry (Alinity c)

Metabolites, enzymes, lipids, proteins, electrolytes & special chemistry panels

💉

Immunoassay (Alinity i)

Chemiluminescent microparticle immunoassay (CMIA) for hormones, cardiac markers & infectious disease

⚙️

Middleware & Connectivity

LIS integration, result validation, reflex testing and data management

🛡️

Quality Control & Safety

Westgard rules, Levey-Jennings charts, calibration workflows and biosafety
